## Client Preferences

### Non-Negotiables
- Zero ransomware risk tolerance
- Immutable backups required
- ZTNA remote access (no traditional VPN)
- Dual-site DR (Site A + Site B)
- ISO 27001 compliance alignment

### Workflow Characteristics
- **Proxy-first editing** — Editors work on local proxies
- NAS holds originals and archives only
- No real-time 8K streaming from NAS required
- Signiant Jet for DIT media ingest
- Wasabi cloud tiering for archives (zero egress fees critical)

### Vendor Preferences
- Storage: Synology (operational simplicity valued over raw performance)
- Firewall: Fortinet (ZTNA integration)
- Network: HPE Aruba (VSX stacking)
- Cloud: Wasabi (predictable costs)

---

## Lessons Learned from This Engagement
1. Proxy-first workflows eliminate need for all-flash NAS
2. Synology DSM significantly reduces operational overhead vs. enterprise storage
3. Wasabi pricing (₹498/TB/month, zero egress) highly attractive vs. AWS/Azure
4. ZTNA approach well-received for remote workforce security
