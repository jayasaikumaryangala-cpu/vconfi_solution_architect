# Pricing History

Track actual pricing received from vendors/distributors for accurate future quotes.

Each pricing entry follows this format:

```markdown
# <Device/Product Name>

- **Last Updated:** <date>
- **Source:** <distributor name, website, or quote reference>

## Price History

| Date | Model | Vendor/Source | Price (INR) | Notes |
|------|-------|--------------|-------------|-------|
| 2026-03 | FortiGate 100F | Ingram Micro | 3,37,200 | With UTP 3yr bundle |
| 2026-03 | FortiGate 100F | IndiaMART | 2,35,000 | Hardware only |

## Notes
- <discount patterns, bulk pricing tiers, seasonal deals>
- <GST included or excluded>
- <lead time observations>
```

## Why Track This
- WebSearch gives list prices — actual partner pricing is often 15-30% lower
- Tracking helps generate more accurate BOMs
- Shows pricing trends over time
- Helps negotiate better deals for clients
