## Notes

- **Always use Plus series (923+, 1823xs+)** for business — j series lacks performance
- **SSD cache:** Highly recommended for 10+ user environments (+50,000 budget)
- **RAM upgrade:** DS923+ can take 32GB total (16GB ECC SODIMM ~12,000)
- **10GbE add-on:** E10G22-T1-Mini card for DS923+/1823xs+ (~35,000)
- **AMC:** Not typical — Synology provides 3-year warranty; extended available
