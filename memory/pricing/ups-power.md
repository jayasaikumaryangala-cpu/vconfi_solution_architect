## Notes

- **Online double-conversion required for:** Servers, storage, network core
- **Line-interactive acceptable for:** Access switches, workstations, non-critical |
- **Runtime calculation:** At 50% load, runtime (minutes) ≈ (Battery Ah × 12 × 0.8) / Load in A
- **Battery replacement:** Every 3-4 years, cost ~30% of UPS price
- **AMC:** 8-12% of equipment cost annually
- **Installation:** Rackmount UPS need professional installation (~10,000-25,000)
